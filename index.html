Newsletter Visualization

To visualize and compare the newsletter data you can select up to two newsletter campains in Campain index. Click on the list entry for the compain which shell be compared and click again to remove the selection.

The Scatterplot shows the pair of Click Rate and Open Rate for all compains. The selected campains are highlighted here by bigger circles.

You can compare some Performance Indicators with bar charts. The Open Rate, Click Rate and Reading Duration are shown here with grouped bar charts for the selected campains.

On the Location of Recipients map you can see the same performance indifactors mapped to the number of recipients of capitals. The bar charts can be build relative to the region or relative to the whole country. You can change the Performance Indicator by clicking on another of the bar charts.
